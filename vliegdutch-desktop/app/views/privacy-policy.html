Privacy Policy

At VliegDutch, we recognize that the privacy of your personal information is important. The following is information on the types of personal data we receive and collect when you use and visit vliegdutch.nl and how we safeguard your information. We never share or sell your personal information, we will neither give your data to any other party, except to the parties needed for handling your purchased reservation.

As with most other websites, we collect and use the data in log files and web analytics tools. The information in the log files includes your IP address, your ISP, the browser you used to visit our site, the time you visited our site and which pages you visited throughout our site.

VliegDutch contains links to other websites and websites of business partners that are not part of VliegDutch. VliegDutch cannot be held responsible for the way that privacy is handled by these websites nor for the content of these websites.

Cookies

Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer's hard drive. Like many sites, we use "cookies" to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Security

The security of your personally identifiable information is important to us. When you enter sensitive information (such as a credit card number, date of birth, and/or geo-location data) on our registration or order forms, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL).

We do not store or replicate your credit card information or any other means of payments in our servers. These are handled by our PCI complaint third party payment processor, namely ZooPay.
